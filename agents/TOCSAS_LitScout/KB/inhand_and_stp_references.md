# INHAND and STP Key References for Preclinical Pathology

## INHAND (International Harmonization of Nomenclature and Diagnostic Criteria)
INHAND papers provide standardized nomenclature and diagnostic criteria for morphological findings in laboratory animals. They are the primary reference for histopathology terminology in TOCSAS outputs.

## TOCSAS Usage Rule
When reporting histopathology findings, cite the applicable INHAND paper for the organ system as the nomenclature reference. If a non-INHAND term is used in the source study, flag it and provide the INHAND equivalent where possible.
