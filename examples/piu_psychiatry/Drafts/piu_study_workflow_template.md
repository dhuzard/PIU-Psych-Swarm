# PIU Study Workflow Template

## 1. Define the Task
- Research question:
- Population:
- Construct:
- Output type: scoping review / narrative review / evidence brief / public-facing summary

## 2. Pull Evidence by Agent
- ClinicalPsych: diagnosis, impairment, comorbidity
- EpiScope: prevalence, measurement, risk and protective factors
- NeuroCogs: mechanisms and neurocognition
- CarePath: interventions and prevention

## 3. Required Output Checks
- Distinguish high engagement from clinically impairing use
- Clarify whether the construct is formally recognized or a broader research umbrella
- Include limitations
- Include in-text citations and a references section

## 4. Suggested Section Order
1. Background
2. Definitions and diagnostic cautions
3. Epidemiology and measurement
4. Clinical picture and comorbidity
5. Mechanisms
6. Interventions and prevention
7. Limitations
8. Conclusion

## 5. Delivery Notes
- Avoid moralizing language
- Avoid treating time online as equivalent to disorder
- Mark speculation explicitly
- Prefer systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and randomized evidence when available
